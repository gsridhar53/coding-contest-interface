Largest of two numbers

Given two numbers, output the larger of those two numbers

There first line of input contains an integer T - the number of test cases.
Then T test cases follow.
For each test case there will be integers N and M

For each test case output the sum of the two numbers

1000<=T<=1000
-10000<=N<=10000
-10000<=M<=10000

Input:
5
10 20
50 50
0 -1
-20 -30
5 0

Output:
20
50
0
-20
5
